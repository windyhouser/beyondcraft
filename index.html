For Mac:

  1. Download the forge installer here
  2. Go to your finder.
  3. Navigate to your downloads folder
  4. Install forge using the installer (Make sure to run the most recent minecraft first.)
  5. Run the new minecraft profile, which will be named Forge.
  6. Close minecraft
  7. In finder, create a new finder window, and navigate to your minecraft folder.
  8. Look for your mods folder.
  9. Download the mods
  10. Move all the mods from the downloads folder to the mods folder
  11. Run the minecraft profile named forge again. Done

How to be a good BeyondCraft citizen

Rules:

  1. Never grief ANYTHING. Griefing means destroying anything that you did not build and who's owner did not give you permission. The mods will give you 1 warning if you're online. Permanent Ban if you grief twice or at mod's discretion.
  2. NEVER curse. There are children playing in this server. The mods will give you 1 warning if you're online. Permanent Ban if you curse twice or at mod's discretion.
  3. Never steal. The mods will give you 2 warning if you're online. Permanent Ban if you steal three times or at mod's discretion.
  4. Be polite. I know this sounds silly, but sometimes you might need to say please. Even if you don't curse, that doesn't mean you can't be rude. Temporary Ban if you're rude.
  5. No pretending to be a mod. The admin knows all the mods personally, so they will know if you do. Permanent Ban
  6. NO derogatory terms. No saying things like "Girls are stupid." Or "Gay people suck." You will be banned. We are accepting of all here. That doesn't mean you can't have personal opinions, but please keep them to yourself.
  7. No advocating products. This server's only purpose is to provide enjoyment. NOT to tell people about your server.
  8. No behaving inappropriately. You can never know who is really at the computer. Just because the person's skin is female, doesn't mean they are. Be smart.

Please report all rule breakers. Thank you, for being a considerate BeyondCraft Citizen.
